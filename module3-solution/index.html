Beef

You know skirt steak as the star of beef fajitas. But this scrumptious cut is no one-hit wonder. Skirt steak is the budget-friendly beef that brings big flavor, particularly when it's marinated and grilled. Try these diverse preparations, and you'll wonder why you ever spent heaps more on the expensive steaks. And don't forget to cut skirt steak against the grain. The meat will be so much more tender.
